### **Our Beliefs**
**URL:** `/about/beliefs`

**Content:**
```
# Our Beliefs

## Core Doctrines

### 1. The Bible
We believe the Bible is the inspired, infallible Word of God and our 
ultimate authority for faith and practice.

### 2. The Trinity
We believe in one God, eternally existing in three persons: Father, 
Son, and Holy Spirit.

### 3. Salvation
We believe salvation is a gift of God's grace, received through faith 
in Jesus Christ alone.

### 4. The Church
We believe the Church is the body of Christ, called to worship God, 
make disciples, and serve the world.

### 5. Baptism & Communion
We practice water baptism by immersion and celebrate Holy Communion 
as ordained by Christ.

### 6. The Second Coming
We believe in the personal, visible return of Jesus Christ.

## Our Values

✝️ **Faith** - Trusting God in all circumstances
❤️ **Love** - Showing Christ's love to all
🙏 **Prayer** - Seeking God's presence continually
📖 **Scripture** - Living by God's Word
🤝 **Community** - Growing together in Christ
🌍 **Mission** - Reaching the lost for Jesus
```

## 🎯 Implementation Notes

### To Create These Pages:

1. **Create page files** in `app/` directory:
   ```
   app/about/story/page.tsx
   app/about/leadership/page.tsx
   app/about/beliefs/page.tsx
   etc.
   ```

2. **Use consistent layout** with:
   - Hero section with page title
   - Content sections
   - Call-to-action buttons
   - Related links

3. **Add animations** using the animation system

4. **Include images** for visual appeal

5. **Make it responsive** for all devices
